Meet Attendance Tracker - for Google Meet

Made specially for teachers and digital teams

How to use

  1. Join a meeting in meet.google.com

  2. Once you have joined a meeting, you will see a new button Track Attendance at the top center of the Google Meet screen.

  3. Click the Track Attendance button to start tracking attendance. You can also view the tracking duration.

  4. Click the same button again to generate the attendance report, which includes the participant names and their attendance duration.
